URANUS

- Ice giant with a bluish tint due to methane.
- Tilted on its side, rotates almost perpendicular to its orbit.
- Rings are less prominent compared to Saturn's.
- Has 13 faint rings and unique features like the "Uranus Collar."
- Voyager 2 discovered small moons around Uranus.
- Uranus has a set of narrow, dark rings, composed of organic compounds.
- Atmosphere mainly consists of hydrogen, helium, and methane.
- Miranda, a moon of Uranus, has a varied and complex terrain.
- Tidal forces from Uranus' moons contribute to its unique axial tilt.
- Uranus' distance from the Sun is about two-thirds of its distance at aphelion.
