NEPTUNE

- Farthest planet from the Sun, blue giant with strong winds.
- Similar in composition to Uranus.
- Notable moon Triton orbits in the opposite direction of Neptune's rotation.
- Magnetic field undergoes rapid changes.
- Great Dark Spot observed by Voyager 2 has disappeared.
- Neptune's winds are the fastest in the solar system, reaching speeds of up to 1,500 miles per hour.
- Triton, Neptune's largest moon, has a retrograde orbit and potential cryovolcanism.
- Voyager 2 is the only spacecraft to have visited Neptune, passing by in 1989.
- Neptune's atmosphere contains methane, giving it a blue color.
- Neptune's magnetic field is tilted relative to its rotation axis.
