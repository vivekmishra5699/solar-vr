VENUS

- Similar in size to Earth with a thick carbon dioxide atmosphere.
- Hot surface temperatures, hot enough to melt lead.
- Often referred to as Earth's "sister planet."
- Completes an orbit around the Sun in 225 Earth days.
- Tidally locked, rotates slowly, and has retrograde rotation.
- Venus has a super-rotation atmosphere, with winds reaching speeds of up to 224 mph.
- Surface features include volcanic structures and lava plains.
- Explored by various spacecraft, including the Magellan mission.
- Venus Express discovered a massive double-eyed atmospheric vortex at its south pole.
- Venus has phases, similar to the Moon, first observed by Giovanni Zupi in 1639.
